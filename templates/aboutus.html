About CampusConnect

CampusConnect is a student-driven platform built to centralize academic materials, career opportunities, and collaboration tools in one place. It enables students to access notes, previous year papers, and verified opportunities like internships, jobs, and scholarships. It also encourages meaningful student-to-student and student-to-faculty collaboration through projects, research, and mentorships.

Our Mission

Empower every student with equal access to educational materials, career opportunities, and platforms for innovation.

Our Vision

Create a connected student community where resources and mentorship flow freely to help all students thrive.

How CampusConnect Helps

  • Access academic notes and previous year papers
  • Discover internships, jobs, hackathons, and scholarships
  • Collaborate with peers and faculty on projects

CampusConnect is more than a platform — it's a community built by and for students from MKSSS’s Cummins College of Engineering for Women, Pune.
